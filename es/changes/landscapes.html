Landscapes

One exciting thing from landscape is the hiking on a tall mountain.


Key Words in Landscapes

Word Definition
Landscape is the shape of the land surface.
Plains also called lowland. low elevation and flat.
Plateau has variation in elevation. rolling hills. Angular cliffs.
Mountain is the tallest. high elevation

Key Things to Know in Landscapes

  • Bedrock structure is primarily responsible for the landscape.
  • Plateau is a large flat area of land that is higher than other areas of land that surround it.
