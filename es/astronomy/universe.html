Universe

One exciting thing from universe is the recognition of how vast the universe is - beyond our comprehension.



Key Words in Universe

Word Definition
Star a massive object in space that creates and radiates energy.
Nuclear Fusion Stars produce and emit energy by nuclear fusion. In nuclear fusion, smaller atoms are joined together to produce energy
Luminosity brightness of a star.
Big Bang the universe started as a small volume and expanded rapidly.
Cosmic Background radiation is the electromagnetic radiation left over from the formation of the universe.
Doppler Effect Galaxies are moving away from Earth. This moving away shows up as a redshift on spectrum.
Galaxy a huge group of stars held together by gravity.
Milky Way Galaxy our sun is a part of the Milky Way Galaxy.

Key Things to Know in Universe

  • Red shift = moving away from you (universe is expanding), blue or violet shift = moving toward you.
  • The universe began with a big explosion--"The Big Bang." Proof = cosmic background radiation from the explosion.
  • Our solar system is located on one of the outer arms of our Milky Way Galaxy.
