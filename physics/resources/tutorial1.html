Make a Tutorial

Teacher Peter Tsun
School Newtown High School

Brief

Write a web page that explains one of the following concepts: Conversion, scientific notation and operations on numbers in scientific notation, draw vectors, or adding and subtracting vectors.

Requirements:

Your tutorial must:

- be written in html and css and possibly Javascript.
- have a written explanation (text) that explains the concepts.
- have pictures or illustrations that help the readers learn the concepts.
- provide several examples that illustrate how the concepts can be applied to some common problems.
- include relevant videos that explain the concepts in a clear and concise manner. (maybe a Music video)
- provides a way for a reader to leave behind comments and feedback on your page.

Presentation:

You will read another person’s web page that teaches a concept other than the one you chose for your project. You will have 8 minutes to explain the concepts based on what you learned from the other student’s web page. In your presentation, be sure to teach the big ideas in a clear and succinct way and to show how the concepts are applied to solve common problems. If time permits, conclude the presentation with a video or a song about the concept you are presenting.
