App Development for Android Devices

App development allows you to harness the computing power of mobile devices to do some pretty incredible things. To get started, here is a list of laboratory activities that help you become familiar with programming apps for android systems. In order to get into the programming environment of android app, you need a login account with google. Once you have a google account, you can log into App Inventor and start designing and building apps.

There will be times when you figuratively sweat to get your program to work, but the process of finding out the solution is rewarding in itself. By building your own app, you will gain a sense of accomplishment and confidence in yourself.
