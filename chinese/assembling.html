Assembling - Sentences in Chinese 句子

On this page, you will find prompts that ask you to make a sentence in Chinese and pronounce it. In Chinese, "How would you say ...?" is "怎樣說 ...?" Remember to use Google Translate to hear its pronunciation.

How would you say? 怎樣說?

Building Blocks 1: You Me We

I am an American.

我是美國人。

I am Chinese. I am a China person.

我是中國人。

We are all Americans.

我們都是美國人。

How about you?

你呢?

Are you busy?

你忙嗎？

I am not very busy, but I am reading a book now.

我不很忙, 可是我現在看書。

我現在想見你，因為你是我的朋友。

他們說中文。
